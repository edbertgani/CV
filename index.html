Edbert Gani Suryahudaya

Researcher at Centre for Strategic and International Studies

Gani is a researcher in the Department of Politics and Social Change, at CSIS Indonesia. He joined CSIS in 2019.
Before joining CSIS, Gani was involved in establishing the Atma Jaya Institute of Public Policy, where currently, he acts as an advisor to the institute.
Apart from that, he has various experiences as an analyst and consultant for the business and public sector.

Gani graduated from the University of Indonesia, holding a bachelor’s degree in Political Science, and then continued his education at the London School of Economics and Political Science, where he graduated and obtained an MSc in Political Science and Political Economy. He has particular concerns about political economy and voting behavior.

Additionally, he is an active columnist, having published many articles on several distinguished national media platforms.
